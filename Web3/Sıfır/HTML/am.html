Av Mevsimi

2hr. 12mn.
Detective





Director : Yavuz Turgul



Actors : Şener Şen, Cem Yılmaz, Çetin Tekindor



imDb : 8.1

Ferman (Şener Şen), a competent police officer, is a small-scale legend in the entire organization with his years of experience, remarkable intuition and expert follow-up of his subjects. They refer to Ferman among themselves as "Hunter". Another figure of the organization attracts attention as "Crazy" Idris (Cem Yılmaz), who lives up to his nickname with just his looks. This crazy duo are two police officers working in the homicide unit, as close as father and son. Anthropology graduate, quiet Hasan (Okan Yalabık) wanders among them as a newly joined "Novice". A murdered young girl will bring them face to face with drug dealer Asit, one of Turkey's wealthiest men, Battal Çolakzade (Çetin Tekindor), the girl's older brothers Abbas, Vakkas and many other men. Ferman, who devoted his life to his wife and struggles with her malady, Idris, who is madly devoted to his ex-wife Asiye (Melisa Sözen), and Hasan, a new graduate and wandering around the world like a tourist... The murder they are trying to solve will never make the lives of these three police officers the same again...

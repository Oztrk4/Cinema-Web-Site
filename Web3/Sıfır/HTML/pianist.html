The Pianist

2hr. 14mn.
Drama





Director : Ronald Harwood, Roman Polanski



Actors : Adrien Brody, Thomas Kretschmann, Emilia Fox



imDb : 9.0

The Pianist is about a real drama that took place during the Second World War. Adapted from the book of the same name, which tells the memories of the famous Polish pianist Wladyslaw Szpilman, the film focuses on the struggle of the famous pianist, who somehow escaped going to a prison camp, to survive in the slums of Warsaw, at a time when it became impossible to live in Nazi-occupied Poland. The musician, who lives a truly miserable life in the slums, will struggle heroically with other people, even though he is exposed to famine and humiliation. When the day comes and he has the chance to escape from there, he will take shelter in the ruins of the capital and regain his hope with help that comes unexpectedly. The film is one of the strongest works in the filmography of Adrien Brody, who received the Oscar for Best Actor.

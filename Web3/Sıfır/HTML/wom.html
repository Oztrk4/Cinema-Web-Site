Vengeance Time tells the story of a mysterious man who tries to avenge his son. H is a wealthy and powerful man. However, despite all this, he starts working as a private security guard in an armored vehicle company that moves millions of dollars every week. H prevents robbery attempts thanks to his extraordinary abilities. Everyone is happy with his work, but no one knows why he joined this company. H's only reason for working at the company is to take revenge on those who caused his son's death.

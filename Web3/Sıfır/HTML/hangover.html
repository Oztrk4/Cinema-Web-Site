The Hangover 3

1hr. 32mn.
Comedy





Director : Todd Phillips



Actors : Bradley Cooper, Ed Helms, Zach Galifianakis



imDb : 8.5

Two years have passed since that famous night when the wolf pack destroyed Bangkok. Phil, Stu and Doug live in their own house, carefree and 'event-free'. Old files are closed, tattoos have been delivered to the laser beam. Leslie Chow, the lightning rod for trouble, is in a prison in Thailand. While the three men of the herd live an almost normal life, Alan is of course not happy with this situation. After troublesome nights, Alan stopped taking his medications and abandoned himself to nature, which for Alan means a life without limits, without common sense, without filters! Caught in the middle of a personal crisis, our hero finally decides to get help, but the most important responsibility falls on the shoulders of his 3 best friends! The third and last movie in the Hangover movie series, there is no wedding or bachelorette party this time. While the film is directed by Todd Phillips, "Wolfpack" again stars Bradley Cooper, Ed Helms, Justin Bartha and Zach Galifianakis.

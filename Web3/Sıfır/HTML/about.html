HISTORY

Samet Sinema GROUP Samet Sinan Group; It was established in 2001 with the philosophy of creating a different and unique cinema experience in Turkey by combining technology and comfort with a service approach that exceeds expectations. Samet Sinan Group, which was deemed worthy of the "International Cinema Group of the Year" award by UNIC (International Union of Cinemas), the world's largest cinema association, in 2013, with the service and technological innovations it offers, is Turkey's largest cinema company with 79 cinema operations in 30 cities and the number of 658 halls. It is a large cinema chain. Samet Sinemas, which always follows the developments in cinema technologies closely and introduces these innovations to its audience first, aims to maximize the cinema experience of its guests by coming together with UCS in 2016. With this passion, UCS became the first cinema chain in Turkey to complete its digital transformation, that is, to digitize all its image and sound systems. Samet Sinema Group, which is the first company to introduce advanced film technologies such as 4DX, Starium, ScreenX, IMAX to Turkey, also reveals its difference with its Gold Class and Tempur theaters with comfortable, customized seats. World-class technology with Samet Sinema halls; Combining comfort, ergonomics and superior service, Samet Cinema Group aims to bring the pleasure of cinema to everyone with its advantageous campaigns and Samet Sinema Club. In addition to its cinema management activities, Samet Group operates in the cinema advertising sector with Nişantaşı University and in the film distribution sector with Acun Media.

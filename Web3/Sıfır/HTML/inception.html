Inception

2hr. 21mn.
Science Fiction





Director : Christopher Nolan



Actors : Leonardo DiCaprio, Marion Cotillard, Elliot Page



imDb : 9.6

Leonardo DiCaprio stars in this production as "Dom Cobb", a very talented thief. His specialty is extracting and stealing valuable secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dreaming moment, when the mind is at its darkest and most vulnerable. Cobb's talent, this rare skill that can be seen in these rare people, has made him a sought-after player in the dangerous new world of corporate espionage. It also made him an international fugitive and cost him everything he loved. Cobb is offered an opportunity to escape his situation. One last job that could give him his life back; Of course, if he can complete the impossible 'beginning'. Instead of the perfect heist, Cobb and his team of professionals have to pull off the opposite; Their job is not to steal an idea but to plant it. If they succeed, it will be the perfect crime.

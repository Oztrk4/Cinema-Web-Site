We're going back to the violent and crime-filled streets of Jakarta! Two hours have passed since the 'raid' in the first movie and Rama has risked everything to protect his family. Using his cunning and combat skills, Rama infiltrated the criminal gang that the police did not dare to chase. The only thing that will enable him to completely save his family is to be thrown into the middle of a bloody war once again. Once again, he makes new contacts and encounters corruption within the police department... The work, which is the sequel to the movie 'The Raid: Redemption', tells the violent and action-packed story of Rama, who only wants to return home knowing that his family is safe and saved and tries to prove that no mafia group in the world is invincible.

Katniss Everdeen faces President Snow while she is in a fully prepared war with the people of Panem. To help the people of Panem win their fight for freedom, Katniss's close friends Gale, Finnick, Peeta and a group leave District 13, risking their lives. The purpose of this dangerous mission is to assassinate President Snow. The only thing on President Snow's mind is destroying Katniss. Enemies, dangerous traps and life choices will be more challenging than ever in this struggle. Katniss, who has been fighting to survive until now, realizes that she can now shape the future. It is the fourth film of The Hunger Games series, the first film of which was met with great interest. After the first movie, the series changed its directorship and was left under the control of Francis Lawrence, the director of I Am Legend. The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 2, written by Peter Craig and Danny Strong, stars Oscar winner Jennifer Lawrence, Josh Hutcherson, Liam Hemsworth, Woody Harrelson, Elizabeth Banks, Oscar winner Philip Seymour Hoffman, Jeffrey Wright, Willow Shields, Featuring Sam Claflin, Jena Malone, Stanley Tucci and Donald Sutherland. The actors who joined the series in The Hunger Games: Mockingjay Part 1 are Oscar winner Julianne Moore, Mahershala Ali, Natalie Dormer, Wes Chatham, Elden Henson and Evan Ross.

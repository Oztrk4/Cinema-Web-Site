Lilo and Stitch

1hr. 13mn.
Animation





Director : Dean DeBlois, Chris Sanders



Actors : Daveigh Chase, Chris Sanders, Tia Carrere



imDb : 7.2

One of the most popular aliens in the galaxy comes to earth. Off the coast of Hawaii, he comes face to face with a quiet girl named Lilo. Lilo mistakes this creature she encounters for a small puppy and takes it with her and names it Stitch. However, Stitch is the product of a laboratory on another planet. Despite all his foreignness, Stitch will gain the admiration, respect and love of the earthlings.

The Saw

1hr. 51mn.
Horror/Mystery





Director : James Wan



Actors : Leigh Whannell, Cary Elwes, Danny Glover



imDb : 6.9

In the movie, an extremely unusual serial killer has an obsession with explaining the meaning and value of life to his victims. They give themselves a chance to survive by playing deadly games. While the victims, who must make impossible choices, do their best to fight, they unknowingly witness what they can sacrifice to live. The last victims of this killer are Adam and Dr. Lawrence Gordon. These men don't know each other at all, but when they open their eyes, they are in the same room. Another stranger lies on the ground covered in blood. The man committed suicide by shooting himself in the head. They try to figure out what's going on using clues. As of now they have eight hours. At the end of this period, either one of them will die or the killer will kill both of them.

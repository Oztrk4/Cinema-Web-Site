Avengers: Infinity War is about the struggle of heroes who have to join forces against the greatest threat the world has ever seen. After the events between Captain America and Iron Man, our heroes are divided and blown away from each other. All of them are trying to protect the world with their own supporters. But the fate of the world is once again in danger. Thanos, who is after the infinity stones, an unlimited source of power, is the biggest threat the world has ever seen. The fate of humanity is once again in the hands of our heroes who have sworn to fight for humanity. To combat this threat, which no superhero can defeat alone, the teams must unite and confront the threat with all their might...

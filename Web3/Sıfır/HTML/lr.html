Love,Rosie

1hr. 42mn.
Romantic/Comedy





Director : Christian Ditter



Actors : Lily Collins, Sam Claflin, Christian Cooke



imDb : 8.7

Rosie and Alex have been best friends since they were 5 years old; That's why there was no possibility of love and being lovers between them. But whenever it came to choosing a lover, they became each other's worst enemies. A choice made at the age of 18, a missed opportunity, takes their lives in completely different directions. But the bond between them cannot be defeated by miles of distance and passing time. Meanwhile, there are also disappointing relationships, marriages, and divorces... Will they have a second chance when they find each other again, or will they be too late for everything? Adapted from Cecelia Ahern's bestselling novel "Where Rainbows End", the film is directed by Christian Ditter and stars Sam Claflin and Lily Collins.

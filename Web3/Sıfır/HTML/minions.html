Minions

imDb : 7.9

Minions tells the story of Gru, who tries to join the Vicious 6 team, which is notorious for their evil. Growing up in the suburbs, Gru's biggest dream is to be bad enough to join the group of villains known as the Bad 6. Taking action for this purpose, Gru receives the support of the Minions, who are his loyal followers and create chaos in the process. Using their skills, Kevin, Stuart, Bob, Otto and Gru build their first shelter together, experience their first weapon and successfully complete their first mission. Meanwhile, legendary fighter Wild Knuckles, the leader of the Bad 6, is dismissed. Gru also meets with them to become a new member of the group. However, the meeting does not go as well as he expected, and Gru finds himself the group's mortal enemy as he outsmarts them. Using Wild Knuckles as a guide while escaping, Gru discovers that even bad guys need a little help from their friends.
